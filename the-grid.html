🔲 The Grid
Race to empty the deck before all 9 piles go face down.

Setup

Deal one card face-up to each of the 9 piles in a 3×3 grid. The remaining deck sits in the middle.

Taking a Turn

  1. Pick any face-up pile — except the one played on the previous turn (shown in blue as ⏳ resting — it's fine, nobody got it wrong, it just needs a turn off).
  2. Guess whether the next card from the deck will be Higher, Lower, or Same rank as that pile's top card.
  3. Flip the top card of the deck onto your chosen pile.

Outcomes

  • Correct (Higher/Lower): The new card becomes the pile's top card. Next player goes.
  • Correct (Same): The new card becomes the top card AND you choose any face-down pile to flip back face-up.
  • Wrong: The pile flips face-down. You drink.

Win / Lose

  • All 9 piles face-down before the deck runs out → everyone chugs their entire drink.
  • Deck runs out before all piles go face-down → everyone wins! Take as many celebratory drinks as desired.
