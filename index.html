Card Drinking Games

Pick a game, gather your crew, and shuffle up. Rules are built right in — no rulebook required.

👑
King's Cup
Draw a card, follow the rule. Each card value has a unique action — waterfalls, categories, rhymes, and more. Every King makes a house rule and gets tucked under the beer can's tab — whoever cracks it chugs the middle drink.
3–10 players 🔥 Classic
🎯
F*** the Dealer
Guess the rank of the top card. Right on the first try — dealer drinks 3. Wrong twice — you drink the difference. The dealer stays until 3 players in a row miss both guesses.
3–8 players 💀 Heavy
🔲
The Grid
A 3×3 grid of face-up piles. On your turn, pick a pile and guess Higher, Lower, or Same as the next card. Wrong — the pile flips down and you drink. Empty the deck before all 9 piles go down to win. Ace is always high.
2–6 players 🎲 Strategic
🎭
Party Prompts
A Picolo-style prompt card game — dares, duels, votes, confessions, and house rules that stick around. Skipping always costs more than doing it. Supports custom prompts.
3–8 players 🎭 Social
🚌
Ride the Bus
Three phases: deal cards round by round with guessing questions, flip the pyramid to give (or take) drinks and pass cards, then the player holding the most cards rides the bus.
3–8 players 💀 Heavy
🍻 Please drink responsibly. These games are intended for adults of legal drinking age. Know your limits.
