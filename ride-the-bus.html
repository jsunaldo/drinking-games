🚌 Ride the Bus
Three phases: deal cards, flip the pyramid, then the loser rides the bus.

Phase 1 — Deal

Go around the table. Each player answers 4 questions about the next card to be dealt to them. Right → give a drink. Wrong → take a drink:

  1. Red or Black?
  2. Higher or Lower than your first card?
  3. Inside or Outside the range of your first two cards?
  4. What suit? (♠ ♥ ♦ ♣)

After all 4 questions, the card is revealed and they get to keep it.

Phase 2 — The Pyramid

Build a face-down pyramid: 5 cards on the bottom, then 4, 3, 2, 1. Flip cards row by row from the bottom up:

  • Row 1 (bottom, 5 cards): GIVE 1
  • Row 2 (4 cards): TAKE 2
  • Row 3 (3 cards): GIVE 3
  • Row 4 (2 cards): GIVE 4
  • Row 5 (top, 1 card): GIVE 5

GIVE rows: if a flipped card matches a rank in your hand, you give that many drinks AND pass the matching card to another player — whoever receives it takes the drinks.

TAKE row: if it matches, you take the drinks yourself and keep your card.

If a flipped card matches nobody's hand, a new card is automatically drawn from the deck on top of it until someone matches.

Phase 3 — Ride the Bus

The player with the most cards in hand after Phase 2 rides the bus. Ties: most cards of same suit. Still tied: both ride.

The rider must survive one card per card in their hand. Card 1: Red or Black. Every card after: Higher or Lower than the previous card. Get one wrong → drink and restart from card 1. Must get all correct in a row to get off the bus.
