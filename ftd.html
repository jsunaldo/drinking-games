🎯 F*** the Dealer
Guess the rank. The dealer stays until 3 players in a row miss both guesses.

Setup

One player is the Dealer. Play goes clockwise — the Dealer does not guess.

Gameplay

  1. Click any rank column on the board to guess the top card's rank.
  2. Correct on 1st guess: Dealer drinks 3. Streak resets to 0.
  3. Wrong on 1st guess: Higher or Lower is revealed. Guess again by clicking a valid column.
  4. Correct on 2nd guess: Dealer drinks 1. Streak resets to 0.
  5. Wrong on 2nd guess: Player drinks the difference. Streak advances by 1.

Changing the Dealer

3 consecutive players miss both guesses → Dealer passes the deck clockwise.

Deck runs out of cards → Current dealer must chug their drink, then deck reshuffles.

Rank Values

2–10 face value, J=11, Q=12, K=13, A=14 (always high)
