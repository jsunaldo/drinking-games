👑 King's Cup
Flip cards around the beer can. Every King gets tucked under the tab — and sooner or later one of them cracks it.

Setup

All 52 cards are spread face-down in a circle around a beer can in the middle. Players sit around the table and take turns clockwise.

Taking a Turn

Click any face-down card in the circle to flip it and reveal its rule. Follow the rule, then pass to the next player.

Card Rules

Kings & the Can 🍺

Every King drawn = the drawer makes a new house rule everyone must follow, and tucks that King under the tab of the can in the middle — just like real life.

Each King bends the tab a little more. You never know which King will pop it — but once the tab cracks, whoever drew that King chugs the middle drink. It could be the 2nd King… it could be the 4th.
