👑 King's Cup
Flip cards around the beer can. Each card you pass gets tucked under the tab — and sooner or later one of them cracks it.

Setup

All 52 cards are spread face-down in a circle around a beer can in the middle. Players sit around the table and take turns clockwise.

Taking a Turn

Click any face-down card in the circle to flip it and reveal its rule. Follow the rule, then pass to the next player.

Card Rules

Kings

Every King drawn = the drawer makes a new house rule everyone must follow for the rest of the game. Anyone who breaks a rule drinks.

The Can 🍺 (choose a mode at setup)

🎲 Random crack: each time you pass, your card is wedged under the can's tab. Every card raises the odds of a crack (starts tiny, guaranteed by the 52nd). Whoever's card cracks the tab chugs the whole can — just like real life.

👑 4th King chugs: the classic rule — the player who draws the 4th King chugs the can.
