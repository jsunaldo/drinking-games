Setup

All 52 cards are spread face-down in a circle around the red cup. Players sit around the table and take turns clockwise.

Taking a Turn

Click any face-down card in the circle to flip it and reveal its rule. Follow the rule, then pass to the next player.

Card Rules

Kings

Every King drawn = the drawer makes a new house rule everyone must follow for the rest of the game. Anyone who breaks a rule drinks.

The player who draws the 4th King must chug a full new beer.
